How the GRE Quant section adapts
  • Both sections count toward your scaled 130–170 Quant score.
  • If you do well on Section 1, Section 2 will be harder — but each correct answer on a hard section is worth more.
  • Within a section: navigate freely, mark for review, change answers up until you submit the section.
  • Once a section is submitted you cannot return to it.
